Hyper Light Drifter Gearbit Locator

Finished the game? Thought you collected all the gearbits? Didn't get the 'Shine Bright' achievement? Never fear! Upload your save file into this locator tool. This tool will help you find those missing gearbits. Huge thanks to Tteraj42 for his super-useful video guides.
